# TCG Coming Soon — Design Brainstorm

## Brand Context
TCG (The Cox Group) is a unified North American logistics brand encompassing trucking, warehousing, and supply chain solutions across the U.S., Mexico, and Canada. The brand palette is deep navy blue (#0D2240), bright yellow-gold (#F5C400), and white. The TCG wordmark features bold italic sans-serif letters with a yellow chevron/arrow motif.

**Idea A — Industrial Command Center**

- **Design Movement:** Neo-Industrial / Corporate Command
- **Core Principles:** Authority through scale, motion-forward, data-driven confidence, geographic reach
- **Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0D2240) as the dominant ground — commanding, trustworthy. Yellow-gold (#F5C400) as a kinetic accent — speed, forward motion. White for legibility. No gradients except subtle dark-to-navy transitions.
- **Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed dark hero with the TCG logo anchored top-left; headline punches diagonally across the viewport; truck image bleeds edge-to-edge with a slight parallax; brand logos scroll horizontally in a marquee strip.
- **Signature Elements:** Yellow chevron dividers between sections; animated countdown clock with industrial-style segmented digits; horizontal rule lines in yellow flanking section labels.
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal yellow underlines; CTA button uses a fill-sweep animation; scroll triggers fade-in-up for each section.
- **Animation:** Entrance — logo slides in from left (200ms ease-out); headline words stagger in 80ms apart; truck image scales subtly from 1.02 to 1.0 on load. Continuous — marquee logos scroll at 40px/s.
- **Typography System:** Headlines in "Barlow Condensed" ExtraBold Italic (mirrors the TCG wordmark energy); body in "Barlow" Regular; labels in "Barlow" SemiBold uppercase tracked wide.

**Idea B — Highway Horizon**

- **Design Movement:** American Road / Cinematic Logistics
- **Core Principles:** Vast open-road energy, cinematic photography, geographic pride, bold simplicity

## Selected Approach: **Idea A — Industrial Command Center**

This approach best mirrors the brand energy visible in the TCG mockups: bold, dark, forward-moving, with yellow-gold as a kinetic accent. The Barlow Condensed typeface closely matches the italic condensed style of the TCG wordmark.
